ESPORT SECURITY

Welcome!

We are eSport Security and we take care of the security around the eSport arenas, both day and night. Our top priority is everybody's safety so we do a lot of preparatory work so everybody has a safe and fun experience while watching their favourite games live.

Our primary duties include: Watching the crowds at the arenas and try to find everybody seats, Working with the eSport teams to make a safe experience for players and other ViPs, Guard expensive equipment from vandalism and theft, Guide lost participants and much more!

Our team are usually 75% regulars but we always like to see new faces so if you have never been to Dreamhack before please dont be afraid to apply!

We're looking for people that can make their own decisions and help when problems arise and always do it with a smile on their faces.

If all of this sounds good and you would like to see some behind the scenes on the e-sport events you have might have seen online, what are you waiting for? Apply today!

In your application please specify why you want to work with esport security, previous similar experiences, who you are and what you work with and if you're comfortable with the night shift. You should expect to hear results from your application within 48 hours. If it's been over 48 hours you can try joining the discord channel and highlight Akira or Smugglarn to ask why.

Volunteers in our team are expected to be at Elmia around 12:00 the 16th june to 18:00 the 20th of june. If you can't be there this early/late please specify why in your application.

If you have any additional questions please join our public discord channel at https://discord.gg/K8hGdYs and we'll answer as soon as possible.

Well met!
